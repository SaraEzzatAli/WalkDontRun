Who are we?

We are a group of four college students who met at RIT hackathon and developed 'walkdontrun', within 24 hours.


What motivated us?

We wanted to focus more on social issues faced by women in our society, which led us to formulate this idea.


More about this website..

This website was originallybased on the idea of promoting security for women, but it has been developed for the welfare of every individual. WalkDontRun not only depicts the shortest but als the safest route that a person could take at any point of time.Created using a heat map, the green in this map shows the safest paths whereas yellow depicts moderately safer pathways and red is straight up DANGER!
